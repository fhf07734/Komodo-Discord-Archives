Few other notes and clarifications about information that we have already shared.

 - The much waited rebrand is going live on July 15th together with the Antara Framework launch.
 - Antara is a framework used to develop customizeable and purpose-built Smart Chains
 - 'Chain Lizard' was an codename for the blockchain generator app. The branded name is "Antara Composer" (Antara Smart Chain Composer)
 - Antara Composer app is used to launch new Smart Chains on AWS infastructure within a minute (new product)
 - AtomicDEX is a multi-coin wallet with atomic swap support. It is currently in closed beta & heavy testing. (new product)

[02-Jul-19 10:43 AM] Mylo#8306
@acidbird hiring process is HR related.   i'm not in HR, however this is how i've seen komodo work with new hires:
 - we will always be ready to collaborate with talented devs with enthusiasm, drive & an exceptional history.   we cannot turn back great devs.   some devs and other techs have approached, and then find their own niche - sometimes it's in kmd, sometimes it's in a related project.  there are dozens of related projects with finite timelines.   and then these devs/techs move onto some other project.   then they drop in & out to fill some technology requirement.
 - when an advert is posted, there is a need to fill a gap for a longer term & hone the skills of the new hire to specific tech or specific new tech usually.   there is a focus on not creating technical debt.  technical debt is not a sustainable way to run a project like kmd, from a HR perspective.
