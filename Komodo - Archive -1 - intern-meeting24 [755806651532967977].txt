[24-Sep-20 12:57 AM] Audo#5667
it might seem confusing at first, but basically there are two suitcases (one from maker and one from taker) and then a public large chest.

2. Maker generates 'swap secret' for the public chest. Once the chest is closed it cannot be opened without having the 'swap secret'. He then puts KMD to a chest and locks it (and only the other party can now open the briefcase).
3. Taker takes his BTC and puts it to the other suitcase that is then locked and only the maker can open it. Then the suitcase goes to the large chest and now the entire chest is closed.
4. Maker can open the large chest with 'swap secret' and can now take his suitcase and open it.
5. Taker comes to the chest that has already been opened and takes the remaining suitcase


[24-Sep-20 12:59 AM] Audo#5667
The whole point to understand (and what this tires to illustorate) is the curcial step #4 where the maker has to use the 'swap secret' to get what he wants, and by doing that he allows the Taker to come in (in step 5) and get his side of the trade.
